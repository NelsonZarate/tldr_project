whoami

[0m[0mPrint the username associated with the current effective user ID.
More information: https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/whoami.

 - [0m[32;23;22;24;25mDisplay currently logged username:
   [33;23;22;24;25mwhoami[0m
[0m
 - [0m[32;23;22;24;25mDisplay the username after a change in the user ID:
   [33;23;22;24;25msudo whoami[0m
[0m[0m
